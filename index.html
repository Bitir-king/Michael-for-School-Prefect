Discover His Story

Early Life & Foundation

Born in 1978 in the mineral-rich lands of Ghana’s Western Region, Michael Kissi grew up in an environment where business and ambition ran deep. His father, a successful entrepreneur in the region, laid the foundation for him to understand the value of hard work, networking, and community support. From a young age, Michael showed a sharp mind for business and an instinct for spotting opportunities — whether in trade, transport, or investments. He was not just smart but strategic. While most young men his age were focused on small ventures, Michael was already learning how to manage people, handle accounts, and make smart financial moves. This mindset would later help him build one of the most powerful business empires in the Western Region.

Rise to Success

Michael Kissi’s story truly began with transportation. He founded Western Transport Services, a company that started with a few vehicles and expanded into one of the largest regional charter bus transport networks with buses, and later added trucks transporting manganese from the quarry to the port. His company became known for reliability, safety, and fair prices — qualities that earned him both respect and trust among the people of Tarkwa and beyond. He then reinvested profits into microfinance, gold refining, real estate, automotive sales, and more.

Business Empire

  • Western Transport Services – Connecting Tarkwa to major cities like Takoradi, Kumasi, and Accra, chartering mining workers to and from the mines. Known for efficiency and customer care.
  • Leverage Microfinance Bank (Company) – Providing financial services and investment opportunities to small businesses and individuals, especially young entrepreneurs and women..
  • Germm Kissi Company Limited – One of his companies, that parent other small businesses and one is example is SKY 40-40.
  • B & M Pipes – Major supplier of industrial and construction pipes. Backbone of local water infrastructure projects.
  • Sky 40-40 Manufacture – Supplying S.P.C tiles across the country; a leading supplier in the region. Provides jobs to locals. Plays role in ethical production.
  • MK3 Motors – Luxury & commercial vehicle dealership attracting clients from Tarkwa, Takoradi, and beyond.
  • Real Estate & Estates Ownership – Owns modern housing estates for middle class and professionals; helping shape both Tarkwa and Takoradi's skyline.

Wealth & Lifestyle

With an estimated net worth of over $400 million, Michael Kissi stands as one of the most successful businessmen in Ghana’s Western Region. He owns a fleet of luxury cars — SUVs, sports cars, executive vehicles — used both for business and leisure. Despite his wealth, he remains humble and rooted. He is also a devoted father of four with his lovely wife MISS. REBECCA KISSI, supporting and guiding them to follow his values of discipline, vision, and leadership.

Philanthropy & Community Impact

Michael Kissi’s success is matched by generosity. He frequently donates to schools, hospitals, and churches in Tarkwa and Takoradi. His scholarship programs help many young students continue their education. He also supports infrastructure development in rural areas. “Success means nothing if your community remains behind,” He stated in one of his recent interviews.

Vision & Legacy

Michael Kissi continues to expand his empire across Ghana, with future plans to enter oil distribution and international trade. His vision is to build a business legacy that lasts for generations and uplifts countless families through employment and empowerment.
